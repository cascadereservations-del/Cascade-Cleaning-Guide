🧺 Laundry — Step‑by‑Step (Precision Protocol)

Follow this order for consistent results. Whites are boosted with correctly dosed oxygen bleach; all loads use HE enzyme detergents with precision.

1️⃣ Sort & Pre‑Treat

Main use: Prevent color transfer; attack stains before heat sets them.
  • Separate: whites / lights / darks. Wash towels separately from sheets.
  • Pre‑treat with enzyme spray or paste (detergent + a little water).
  • Inspect hems, pillowcases, corners for makeup or oil marks.
💡 Pro tip: Apply pre‑treat on the back of the stain and dab from the front to push soil out.

2️⃣ Choose Detergent (HE Enzyme)

Main use: Remove body oils/food soils efficiently with low suds.
  • Surf Professional / Surf Matic — ~2 tbsp liquid or 1–1.5 scoops powder for 7–8 kg (follow label).
  • Powerclean HE Liquid — 25–40 mL for 7–8 kg; add an extra rinse in hard water.
  • Ariel / Breeze (HE enzyme variants) — good alternatives if stocked; verify enzymes (protease, amylase, lipase) on label.
  • HE in non‑HE washers is fine — use more water if over‑sudsing.

Enzyme names to look for: protease (protein), amylase (starch), lipase (oils). These biological agents break down soil particles, making them key for professional cleaning.

4️⃣ The No‑Conditioner Protocol

CRITICAL PROTOCOL: We **ELIMINATE** the use of traditional liquid fabric conditioner.

Why we do not use fabric conditioner:

  • Residue & Build-up: Conditioners coat fibers with waxy residue, which builds up in the washer (tub, hoses), leading to **Biofilm and Musty Odors**—a major anti-cleaning effect.
  • Reduced Absorbency: The waxy coating clogs fabric micro-loops, **significantly reducing the absorbency** of towels and bedding.
  • Machine Health: Over time, the residue promotes soap scum, necessitating more frequent and harsh machine cleaning.
The Alternative: Laundry Finishing Spray

Apply a **Fabric Cologne or Laundry Finishing Spray** (e.g., *Powerclean Fabric Cologne*) after linens are dry and before folding. This delivers scent and crispness without residue.

5️⃣ Brighten Whites (Oxygen Bleach)

Main use: Restore brightness without chlorine damage.
  • **For Dosage:** Refer to the **Dosing Calculator (Step 3)** for the precise cap/mL recommendation based on load weight.
  • Soak: 1–2 tbsp per 4 L warm water, 20–30 min; wash as usual.
  • Boost: add the calculated dose to main wash for white towels/sheets.
  • Spot paste: apply 5–10 min then rinse; do not let dry on fabric.
⚠️ Safety: Zonrox OxyBleach cap is approximately 5 mL. **DO NOT USE THE ZONROX BOTTLE CAP FOR CHLORINE BLEACH DOSING**; use a dedicated measuring cup/syringe.

6️⃣ Temperature & Cycle

  • Whites: hot (60°C) • Lights: warm (40°C) • Darks: cold (30°C).
  • Choose normal/regular cycle for most linens; delicate for pillowcases if needed.
  • Spin at medium to reduce wrinkles before drying.
🌀 Pro tip: A short cool rinse at the end can reduce set‑in wrinkles on cotton. Always dry bedding immediately to prevent mildew odors.

🛋️ Sofas & Carpets — Odor‑Free Comfort

Prevent first; deep‑clean only when needed to protect fabrics and indoor air.

Prevention

Main use: Keep fabrics fresh between turnovers.
  • Vacuum weekly incl. seams/edges; use crevice & upholstery tools.
  • Open windows for cross‑ventilation after checkout (15–20 min).
  • Enforce no‑smoking/no‑pets inside the unit.
  • Light fabric spray only when cushions are fully dry.
🌬️ Pro tip: Run an air purifier on low during cleaning to reduce dust and odors. Replace filters monthly during peak season.

Deep Clean & Deodorizing Sprays

Main use: Neutralize organic odors using targeted, local products.

General Methods:

  • Baking Soda Method: Sprinkle baking soda → 30–60 min dwell → vacuum thoroughly.
  • Fabric Refreshers: Widely available sprays like **Febreze or Lysol Fabric Refresher** use cyclodextrin to trap and neutralize odors on sofas/curtains.
  • White Vinegar Solution: A simple 1:1 **White Vinegar and Water** solution applied via fine mist is an inexpensive and highly effective neutralizer.
  • Spills: Blot (don’t rub). Test mild detergent + water on hidden spot.

Local Deodorizer Sourcing (Enzymatic):

  • Product: Look for **Pawsafe** or **Wonderhome Naturals** online (Shopee/Lazada). These are the most highly-rated, strong **Enzyme Cleaners** that break down organic odors.

Cheap Alternative: Distilled White Vinegar. Neutralizes organic odors but lacks the enzyme's ability to fully break down proteins. Spray a diluted solution (1:1 water/vinegar).

⚠️ Safety: Follow device manual; never run ozone with people or pets present. Use an ozone detector to verify safe air levels upon re-entry.

🧼 General Cleaning — Kitchen, Sinks, Drains

Use fit‑for‑purpose cleaners, respect dwell times, and ventilate well.

Kitchen Degreaser

Main use: Cut oil/grease on stovetops, tiles, counters.
  • Mr. Muscle Kitchen Cleaner: spray → 2–3 min dwell → wipe damp → rinse.
  • Alternative: Any kitchen‑safe degreaser labeled for food‑contact surfaces (rinse required).
🔥 Pro tip: Warm water cloth after dwell removes stubborn film faster. For heavy build-up, use a plastic scrubber.

🧫 Preventing Yellowing and Mold Stains

PHILIPPINE CONTEXT: Preventative maintenance is critical against high humidity.

Yellowing (Plastics/Grout Protocol)

  • Restoration (Plastics): For already yellowed plastic, apply **Hydrogen Peroxide Cream (Volume 40)**, wrap in plastic, and expose to indirect sunlight (Retr0bright Method).
  • Grout Whitening: Scrub with a paste of **powdered oxygen bleach** and hot water. For severe stains, use a 1:10 solution of **Zonrox : Water** for 5 minutes, then rinse.
  • Prevention: Apply an industrial **Grout Sealer** to prevent mold penetration.

Mold & Mildew (Hard Surfaces Protocol)

  • Elimination: Mix **1 part Zonrox to 10 parts water**. Apply to non-porous surfaces (tiles/grout), dwell for 10 minutes, then rinse *well*.
  • Prevention: **Fix the water source first!** (Leaks/condensation). Run the **exhaust fan for 30 minutes *after* bathing** and wipe down wet walls to control moisture.

Local Sourcing Detail (Specialty Chemicals):

  • Hydrogen Peroxide Cream (Volume 40): Look for brands like **Bremod** or **Natural Colorspa Developer** at local beauty supply stores, Watsons, or online. (It is typically a hair product, 12% concentration).
  • Powdered Oxygen Bleach: Source pure **Sodium Percarbonate** (e.g., from **Powerclean Solutions** on Shopee/Lazada) or use an Oxi-type laundry additive. (This is the gold standard for non-corrosive whitening).

Cheap Alternative for Yellowing: Natural Sunlight & Time. Prolonged exposure to indirect UV light is the principle behind the chemical method, but it is slower and less reliable without the peroxide compound.

Safety Primer 🧤

  • Read labels; never exceed doses; ensure ventilation during and after use.
  • Wear gloves/eye protection for strong cleaners; store sealed and upright.
  • Rinse surfaces before switching products. 🚫 Never mix chlorine bleach with ammonia or acids.
